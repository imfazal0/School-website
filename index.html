About Islamia Inter College, Shahjahanpur

Our Legacy

Established in 1932, Islamia Inter College, Shahjahanpur, has been a cornerstone of quality education for nearly a century. Founded as A.V. Junior High School by Alhaj Khan Bahadur Fazlur Rahaman Khan (Chhote Khan), the institution evolved over the years to become a renowned center for learning. By 1942, it was recognized as an Intermediate College, offering a strong foundation in both Literary and Science subjects.

Our Vision & Mission

At Islamia Inter College, we are committed to academic excellence, character development, and holistic growth. Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, discipline, and values, preparing them for future challenges in a rapidly evolving world.

Academic Excellence

We take pride in offering well-structured academic programs with a dedicated team of experienced educators. Over the years, our institution has produced outstanding scholars, professionals, and leaders who have contributed significantly to society.

Infrastructure & Facilities

Our campus is equipped with modern classrooms, well-stocked libraries, science laboratories, and sports facilities to ensure a comprehensive learning experience. In 2021, a new main entrance gate was inaugurated, symbolizing our commitment to modernization while preserving our rich heritage.
